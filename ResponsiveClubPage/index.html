About Wyohams

Wyohams is an amateur radio club focused on providing communities of Wyoming another method of communication: radio. We help teach the ins and outs of radio technology, and also provide communications services for public events. If you love technology and learning more about other methods of communication, Wyohams is for you!

What is Amateur Radio?

Amateur Radio (Ham Radio) is a popular hobby and service in which licensed Amateur Radio operators (hams) operate communications equipment. But that just barely even begins to scratch the surface! Other sites say, "Ham radio is people...", and yet others tout the Amateur Service as a national resource. We can define Amateur (ham) radio from many perspectives. But, truth be told, ham radio is what you make it.
